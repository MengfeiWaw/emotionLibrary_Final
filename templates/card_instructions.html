We invite you to share your feeling through our playful, interactive platform that ensures your identity stays anonymous.

---------------------------------------------

For Mac users: you may record the screen using the inbuilt Quicktime Player.

File > New Screen Recording > Select the recording area > Record

For Windows users: you may record the screen using the inbuilt Xbox Game Bar.

Suggested recording duration would be around 1 to 2 minutes, but don’t worry if your recording is more or less than that range.
